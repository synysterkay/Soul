# Privacy Policy for Soul Plan

Last updated: November 27, 2025

AppSonics Studio ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Soul Plan mobile application (the "App"). This privacy policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of data when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you use Soul Plan, we collect and process the following personal information:

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number (for authentication and account management)
- **Profile Information**: Gender, date of birth, relationship preferences
- **Location Data**: GPS coordinates and city information to suggest nearby date venues
- **Contact Information**: Phone numbers from your contacts (with your permission) to help you connect with partners in the app
- **Date Planning Information**: Your date preferences, venue selections, and date history
- **Photos**: Profile pictures and date-related images you choose to upload

### Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use our App:

- Device information (type, model, operating system)
- App usage statistics and interaction patterns
- IP address and approximate location
- Crash reports and diagnostic data

### Third-Party Data

We integrate with the following services:

- **Firebase**: Authentication, database, storage, and messaging services
- **Google Sign-In & Apple Sign-In**: Third-party authentication
- **OpenStreetMap**: Venue search and location services
- **Google Mobile Ads**: Advertising services
- **Superwall**: In-app purchase and subscription management

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Create and manage your account
- Provide personalized date planning recommendations using AI
- Connect you with your partner through phone number matching
- Suggest nearby venues and activities based on your location
- Send you notifications about date requests and updates
- Process in-app purchases and subscriptions
- Display relevant advertisements
- Improve our services and user experience
- Ensure the security and integrity of our App

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

- **Service Providers**: Firebase, OpenStreetMap, and other third-party services necessary for app functionality
- **Advertising Partners**: Google Mobile Ads (anonymous usage data for targeted advertising)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely using Firebase cloud services
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

- Access, update, or delete your personal information
- Opt-out of location tracking
- Revoke contacts permission at any time
- Disable push notifications
- Delete your account and associated data
- Opt-out of personalized advertising

To exercise these rights, contact us at dworarena@gmail.com

## Children's Privacy

Soul Plan is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we discover that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

## Permissions Required

The App requests the following permissions:

- **Location**: To suggest nearby date venues (optional but recommended)
- **Contacts**: To help you find and connect with partners (optional)
- **Notifications**: To send you date requests and updates
- **Camera/Photos**: To upload profile pictures and date photos (optional)
- **Internet**: Required for core functionality

## Third-Party Links

Our App may contain links to external websites (Google Maps, venue websites). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites.

## Advertising

We use Google Mobile Ads to display advertisements. These ads may be personalized based on your app usage. You can opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings.

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.
